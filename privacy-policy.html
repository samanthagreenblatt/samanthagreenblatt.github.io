Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026  |  Last updated: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Health Insurance Benefits SMS service ("the Service," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information provided by users ("you") when you interact with our SMS-based health insurance benefits tool.

1. Who We Are

This Service is operated by Samantha Greenblatt as an independent portfolio project. The Service allows users to text a dedicated phone number and receive automated replies about health insurance benefits questions. All messages are inbound — we do not initiate contact or send unsolicited messages.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the Service:

  • Your phone number — provided automatically when you send an inbound SMS.
  • Message content — the text of the questions you send to the Service.
  • Timestamps — the date and time of your messages, for operational and debugging purposes.

We do not collect your name, email address, or any financial or medical records unless you voluntarily include that information in a message.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected through the Service is used solely to:

  • Process your inbound SMS and generate automated responses to your health insurance benefits questions.
  • Maintain and improve the reliability and accuracy of the Service.
  • Diagnose technical issues and monitor for abuse.

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond operating the Service.

4. How We Do Not Use Your Information

We will not:

  • Sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.
  • Share your data with advertisers or marketing platforms.
  • Send you unsolicited messages or marketing communications of any kind.
  • Use your data to build marketing profiles or target you with advertisements.

5. Data Sharing

Your information is not shared with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — We use Twilio to deliver SMS messages. Twilio processes message content and phone numbers as a telecommunications intermediary. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/en-us/legal/privacy.
  • Legal obligations — We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal process.

No other third-party sharing occurs.

6. Data Retention

Message logs may be retained for up to 90 days for operational and debugging purposes, after which they are deleted. You may request earlier deletion by contacting us at the address below.

7. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of the Service entirely by texting STOP to our SMS number.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Service after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
