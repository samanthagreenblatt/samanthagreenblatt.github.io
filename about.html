About me

Asking why before building what.

Hi, I'm Sam.

I'm a Product Manager who loves building things that make complicated systems feel simple. I get energized by finding the real problem, getting the right people in a room, and shipping something that actually works and people use.


Prior to product, I worked in data and analytics, which means I ask a lot of questions before I trust any answer — and I bring that same instinct to every product I build.


Outside of work you'll find me hunting for the best coffee shop in whatever neighborhood I'm in, on the ski slopes (weather permitting), or baking a new batch of sourdough.
